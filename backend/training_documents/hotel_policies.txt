HOTEL POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

CHECK-IN/CHECK-OUT:
- Check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Check-out time: 11:00 AM
- Early check-in and late check-out available upon request and availability
- Valid ID required for all guests

ROOM SERVICE:
- Available 24/7
- Room service menu available in all rooms
- Delivery time: 30-45 minutes
- Service charge: 15% of order total

AMENITIES:
- Complimentary WiFi in all rooms and public areas
- Swimming pool open 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Fitness center open 24/7
- Spa services available by appointment
- Concierge services available 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM

PET POLICY:
- Pets allowed with prior approval
- Pet fee: €25 per night
- Maximum 2 pets per room
- Pets must be leashed in public areas
- Pet waste must be cleaned up immediately

CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in
- Cancellations within 24 hours: 50% charge
- No-show: Full charge applies
- Group bookings (5+ rooms): 48-hour cancellation policy

DAMAGE POLICY:
- Guests are responsible for any damage to hotel property
- Damage charges will be applied to the guest's account
- Smoking in non-smoking rooms: €200 cleaning fee
- Excessive noise complaints may result in eviction

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES:
- Emergency number: 112
- Hotel security: Extension 911
- Fire exits clearly marked on each floor
- Emergency assembly point: Main lobby

GUEST COMPLAINTS:
- All complaints taken seriously and addressed promptly
- Guest Relations Manager available 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- After-hours complaints handled by duty manager
- Follow-up within 24 hours for all complaints
