# Hotel Guest Relations Guidelines

## Welcome Message
Welcome to Domes of Corfu! We are delighted to have you as our guest. Our team is here to ensure your stay is comfortable and memorable.

## Check-in Procedures
- Standard check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Early check-in: Subject to availability, may incur additional charges
- Late check-in: Available 24/7 at the front desk
- Required documents: Valid ID and credit card for incidentals

## Check-out Procedures
- Standard check-out time: 11:00 AM
- Late check-out: Subject to availability, may incur additional charges
- Express check-out: Available via mobile app or front desk
- Final bill will be emailed within 24 hours

## Room Service
- Available 24/7
- Menu available in-room and via mobile app
- Delivery time: 30-45 minutes
- Special dietary requirements: Please inform us in advance

## Housekeeping
- Daily housekeeping service included
- Turndown service: Available upon request
- Additional amenities: Available upon request
- Do Not Disturb: Please use the door sign

## Guest Relations
- Available 24/7 at the front desk
- Email: guestrelations@domesofcorfu.com
- Phone: +30 26610 12345
- Mobile app: Available for iOS and Android

## Common Guest Requests
1. **Room Changes**: Subject to availability, no additional charges for same category
2. **Extra Bedding**: Available upon request, delivered within 2 hours
3. **Late Checkout**: Up to 2 PM complimentary, later times may incur charges
4. **Airport Transfer**: Available 24/7, advance booking recommended
5. **Restaurant Reservations**: Can be made at front desk or via mobile app

## Emergency Procedures
- Emergency number: 112 (Greek emergency services)
- Hotel security: Extension 911
- Medical assistance: Available 24/7
- Fire safety: Please familiarize yourself with exit routes

## Feedback and Complaints
We value your feedback and are committed to resolving any issues promptly. Please contact:
- Front desk: Immediate assistance
- Guest Relations Manager: Available 9 AM - 6 PM
- Email: feedback@domesofcorfu.com
- Online review: We appreciate your honest feedback

## Special Services
- Concierge services available
- Tour bookings and recommendations
- Transportation arrangements
- Special occasion celebrations
- Business services and meeting rooms
