
Domes of Corfu, Autograph Collection – Marriott Policies & Procedures Overview
==============================================================================

Property-Specific Policies & Procedures (Domes of Corfu)
---------------------------------------------------------

1. Check-in / Check-out Times & Requirements
   - Check-in: 15:00 | Check-out: 11:00
   - Early check-in or late check-out upon request, subject to availability.
   - Valid ID and credit card required at check-in. Pre-authorization may apply.

2. Payment & Cancellation
   - Deposit: 2 nights required within 24 hours of booking.
   - Balance due upon arrival.
   - Cash payments over €500 prohibited under Greek law.
   - Free cancellation up to 7 days prior. Within 6 days: 100% charge.

3. Taxes & Fees
   - All rates include VAT, city tax, and service charges.
   - Climate Resilience Tax:
     - €15 per night (April–October)
     - €4 per night (November–March)

4. Pets & Parking
   - Pets: Max one dog or cat (≤ 15 kg), €100 per stay, non-refundable.
   - Parking: Complimentary on-site and valet parking available.

5. Accessibility & Services
   - Fully accessible (elevators, accessible rooms, spa, pools, service animals welcome).
   - Amenities include: free Wi‑Fi for Bonvoy members, concierge, babysitting, spa, pools, fitness center, restaurants, gift shop.

6. Hygiene & Sustainability
   - Marriott-wide cleanliness protocols.
   - Recycling programs for paper, plastic, oil, etc.

Autograph Collection Brand Standards
------------------------------------

1. Brand Philosophy
   - Soft brand: each hotel maintains its own identity and story.
   - Must meet high quality and guest satisfaction standards.

2. Quality Assurance
   - Subject to Brand Standards Audit (BSA) by Marriott.
   - Failures result in re-audits or de-flagging.

Elite Member Benefits at Autograph Resorts
------------------------------------------

1. Lounge / Breakfast / Welcome Gift
   - No lounge: Platinum+ members receive continental breakfast or 750 points.
   - Practices vary widely between properties.

2. Late Check-out
   - Platinum Elite+ members guaranteed 4 PM late check-out EXCEPT at resorts (subject to availability).
   - Guests report inconsistencies at some Autograph properties.
