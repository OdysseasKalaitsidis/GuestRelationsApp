# Email Standards and Templates

## Professional Email Guidelines

### Subject Lines
- Be clear and descriptive
- Use action words when appropriate
- Keep under 50 characters
- Avoid spam trigger words

### Email Structure
1. **Greeting**: Use appropriate salutation based on relationship
2. **Opening**: Brief context or purpose
3. **Body**: Main content with clear paragraphs
4. **Closing**: Professional sign-off
5. **Signature**: Include contact information

### Tone Guidelines
- **Professional**: Formal language, complete sentences
- **Friendly**: Warm but respectful tone
- **Urgent**: Clear priority indicators
- **Apologetic**: Sincere and solution-focused

## Common Email Templates

### Reservation Inquiry Response
**Subject**: Re: Your Reservation Inquiry

Dear [Guest Name],

Thank you for your interest in staying with us at [Hotel Name]. We appreciate you reaching out regarding your reservation inquiry.

[Specific response to their inquiry]

We look forward to welcoming you and ensuring your stay is exceptional. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any additional questions.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Guest Relations Manager
[Hotel Name]
[Contact Information]

### Complaint Response
**Subject**: Re: Your Recent Stay - [Brief Description]

Dear [Guest Name],

Thank you for taking the time to share your feedback about your recent stay with us. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience you experienced.

[Address specific issues mentioned]

We take guest satisfaction very seriously, and your feedback helps us improve our services. We would like to make this right and ensure you have a positive experience with us in the future.

[Proposed solution or compensation]

Please feel free to contact me directly if you have any further concerns or if there's anything else we can do to assist you.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
Guest Relations Manager
[Hotel Name]
[Contact Information]

Guest Relations Team

## Quality Standards

### Response Time
- Acknowledge receipt within 2 hours
- Full response within 24 hours
- Urgent matters: immediate response

### Language Requirements
- Use proper grammar and spelling
- Avoid jargon and technical terms
- Be concise but complete
- Use active voice when possible

### Personalization
- Address guest by name
- Reference specific details from their inquiry
- Use appropriate tone based on situation
- Include relevant hotel information

### Follow-up
- Always offer additional assistance
- Provide clear next steps
- Include contact information
- Set expectations for response time
